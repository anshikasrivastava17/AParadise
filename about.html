In a world of wonder, a small, small girl,
Cute as a button, her hair in a twirl.
An angelic presence, a joy to behold,
With laughter that sparkles, more precious than gold.
Her tiny arms embrace a basket so dear,
A cherub on earth, spreading love far and near.

In a schoolgirl's world of books and pens,
Responsibilities she bears, a weight that lends.
Intelligence sparkles in her eager eyes,
Innocence woven in every thoughtful guise.
Balancing tasks with a heart full of grace,
A beacon of potential, a scholarly embrace.
